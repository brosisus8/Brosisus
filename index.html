More Than a Social Network

Dadadu is a revolutionary short-form video social network app, similar to TikTok but with unique proximity-based matching. Connect with users within 1km radius through three distinct modes: Love ❤️, Business 💼, and Entertainment 🎭. Built on Flutter with Supabase backend, featuring gamified rewards, ephemeral messaging, and AI-powered recommendations.

Dadadu ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Dadadu mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

  • Personal Data: While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including your email address, name, and profile picture.

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, such as encryption and secure server infrastructure (Firebase).
