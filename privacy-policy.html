Dadadu Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

Welcome to Dadadu, a mobile short-form video app developed by BroSisUs. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our app. By using Dadadu, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, profile picture, and other details you provide. For matching features, we may collect location data (with your permission) to enable proximity-based connections within 1 km by default.
  • Content You Create: Videos (up to 20 seconds), comments, likes, and other interactions. Videos are stored in Wasabi and distributed via Bunny CDN.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as modes used (Love, Business, Entertainment), matches, chats, referrals, and moods selected.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and app version for analytics and security.
  • Referral and Diamond Data: Referral links, invited users, and earned diamonds for unlocking features like infinite distance matching or permanent messages.
  • AI-Related Data: When using planned AI features (e.g., content recommendations or moderation), we may process engagement data and video tags.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide core features like video feeds, matching, chats, and referrals.
  • To personalize your experience, such as showing nearby matches or trending videos.
  • To manage badges (e.g., Leaf, ThreeLeaf) and diamonds based on referrals.
  • For security, fraud prevention, and moderation (e.g., AI content filtering).
  • To send notifications and updates via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • For analytics and improvements using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase for authentication and database, Wasabi for storage, Bunny CDN for video delivery, and AI providers (e.g., Groq, DeepSeek) for content recommendations.
  • Matched Users: Profile details and location (anonymized) for matching in Love, Business, or Entertainment modes.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, to protect rights, or in response to legal processes.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular audits. However, no system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of location sharing or notifications.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at brosisus8@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Children's Privacy

Dadadu is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app with the new effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
